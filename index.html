Abhinav Rajagopal

I am a research engineer at Rekindle Automation, where I work on deep learning and embedded systems and tackle challenging problems in sustainability and healthcare.

I've worked on CNNs, Unsupervised learning models in k-means, as well as supervised learning with k-NN, reinforcement models in TDL, and GANs, RBMs. I'm currently involved mainly with tackling unsupervised models including SOMs and applying hierarchical methods to hard problems.

I did my bachelors in EE at SVCE under Anna University.

Research
